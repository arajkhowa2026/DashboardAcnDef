Resolution Time Spread by Tier — P25, Median, P75

Percentile spread of resolution times within each tier. Median is the middle value — half of incidents resolved faster, half slower. P25 is the fastest quarter; P75 is the point below which 75% of incidents fall. A wide P25–P75 gap means highly variable performance; a narrow gap means consistent delivery.

P25 — 25% of incidents resolved within this many hours (fastest quarter)
